## Hook

"So what do you do?" — the current impossibility of explaining your role because the title doesn't exist yet.

## Thesis

We need a name for the integrated practice of designing across humans, agents, and systems. That practice is HAS Design.

## Key Points

- Why existing titles don't fit — not pure UX, not systems architect, not product designer in the traditional sense.
- What HAS Design actually is — designing multi-actor orchestrations.
- Core skills of HAS Designers — UX/UI + systems architecture + protocol design + service design.
- What you're NOT doing vs. what you ARE doing.
- How this relates to HCI (parent) and ACI (sibling).

## Payoff

A role definition that designers can identify with and companies can hire for.
