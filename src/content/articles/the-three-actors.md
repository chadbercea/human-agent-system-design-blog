## Thesis

Designing multi-actor orchestrations requires understanding three distinct actor types, each with different characteristics and design considerations.

## Key Points

- **Humans** are characterized by personas + jobs-to-be-done.
- **Agents** are characterized by archetypes + jobs-to-be-done.
- **Systems** are characterized by specifications + protocols.
- Why each needs different design approaches.
- How they coordinate in orchestrations.

## Payoff

A framework for understanding who and what you're designing for in agentic systems.
