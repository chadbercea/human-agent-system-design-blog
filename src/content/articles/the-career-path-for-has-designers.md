## Hook

"I just bet my career on agentic orchestration." Why you left Docker to lead AI Dev Tools at Atlassian.

## Thesis

HAS Design is the future of product design. Designers who evolve into this practice will lead the next decade of software. Those who don't will be left designing forms.

## Key Points

- Why this is a career-defining shift — not a trend, a fundamental change in how software works.
- What makes someone qualified for HAS Design — deep domain expertise + technical understanding + systems thinking.
- Career progression: UX Designer → Product Designer → HAS Designer → Principal/Staff HAS Designer.
- Why companies need HAS Designers now — agentic products are shipping, teams are confused.
- The Atlassian bet: leading AI Dev Tools as a HAS Designer.

## Payoff

A call to action for designers to evolve, and a recruiting signal for the movement.
