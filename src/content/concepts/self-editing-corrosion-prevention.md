title: Self-Editing Corrosion Prevention
category: design-requirements

synopsis: >
  Humans interacting with capacity-limited partners learn to flatten
  themselves over time. They bring 60% because 100% causes friction. Agents
  do not impose this cost.
framework_implication: >
  HAS-D must account for the fact that agents enable a different
  operational range for the human. Patterns should preserve this property.
  Introducing artificial social dynamics risks reintroducing the corrosion
  the architecture naturally avoids.
